# Lumen — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

## What We Collect

Lumen is designed with privacy at its core. Here is exactly what happens with your data:

**Audio recordings:** Recorded on-device. Not uploaded to any server unless you explicitly choose to save a meeting. Audio is stored locally in your app's private container.

**Transcripts:** Generated on-device using Apple's Speech framework. Sent to our backend only when you save a meeting or request an AI summary.

**AI queries:** When you use LUMEN AI (tap-to-ask), your question and meeting context are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data. We do not store your queries on our servers.

**Voice responses:** Text responses are sent to ElevenLabs to generate audio. ElevenLabs' privacy policy applies. Audio responses are streamed and not stored.

**Subscription status:** Managed entirely through Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment information.

**No account required:** Lumen does not require you to create an account. There is no user database.

## What We Don't Collect

- We don't collect your name, email, or any personal identifier
- We don't sell your data to third parties — ever
- We don't use your meeting content to train AI models
- We don't track your location

## Third-Party Services

- **Anthropic (Claude API):** AI responses. [Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- **ElevenLabs:** Voice synthesis. [Privacy Policy](https://elevenlabs.io/privacy)
- **Apple Speech Framework:** On-device transcription.

## Data Retention

Meeting recordings and transcripts are stored locally on your device until you delete them. If you delete the app, all local data is removed.

## Children

Lumen is not directed at children under 13.
