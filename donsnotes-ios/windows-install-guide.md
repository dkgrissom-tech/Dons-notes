# 🎮 How to put Don's Notes on your iPhone (from a Windows PC)

## Step 1: Get the app file

### Wait for the robot to build it
1. After I push all the code to GitHub, a robot (GitHub Actions) will build the app automatically
2. When it's done, you'll see a download link for **DonsNotes-iPhone.zip**
3. Click it — it downloads to your PC
4. Unzip it. Inside is a file called **DonsNotes.ipa**

> **What's an .ipa?** It's the iPhone app file — like a `.exe` on Windows.

---

## Step 2: Put the app on your iPhone

### You need one free program on your PC

**Choose the easiest option:**

### 🥇 OPTION A: AltStore (Easiest — 5 minutes)
1. Go to **altstore.io** on your PC
2. Download and install **AltServer for Windows**
3. Plug your iPhone into your PC with the USB cable
4. Right-click the AltServer icon in your system tray (bottom right)
5. Click **Install AltStore** → select your iPhone
6. A new app called **AltStore** appears on your iPhone — open it
7. Go to **My Apps** tab → tap **+** in the top corner
8. Pick the **DonsNotes.ipa** file you downloaded
9. **That's it!** Don's Notes appears on your home screen 🎉

### 🥈 OPTION B: Sideloadly (Also free)
1. Go to **sideloadly.io** → download and install
2. Plug in your iPhone
3. Open Sideloadly
4. Drag the **DonsNotes.ipa** file into the window
5. Enter your Apple ID email and password (it's safe — Apple requires this to install)
6. Click **Start**
7. **Done!** Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → tap your profile → tap **Trust**
8. Open Don's Notes from your home screen 🎉
